Cookie Notice

At STREAMIE, we use cookies and other tracking technologies to provide you with a better user experience, improve our services, and deliver personalized content and advertising.

What Are Cookies?
Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit our website or use our app. They allow us to recognize your device and store information about your preferences and behavior.

Why Do We Use Cookies?
We use cookies for several purposes:

Personalization: To deliver personalized content and recommendations based on your viewing history and preferences.
Analytics: To track your usage and behavior on our platform, and to improve our services and content.
Advertising: To deliver targeted advertising and to measure the effectiveness of our ad campaigns.
Security: To protect your account and prevent unauthorized access.
Types of Cookies We Use
We use the following types of cookies:

Session Cookies: These cookies are deleted when you close your browser and are used to store temporary information.
Persistent Cookies: These cookies remain on your device until they expire or are deleted and are used to store information about your preferences and behavior.
Third-Party Cookies: These cookies are set by third-party services, such as Google Analytics, and are used to track your behavior and deliver targeted advertising.
How to Manage Cookies
You can manage cookies in several ways:

Browser Settings: You can adjust your browser settings to accept or reject cookies, or to notify you when a cookie is set.
Cookie Consent: You can opt-out of non-essential cookies by clicking on the "Cookie Consent" button below.
Third-Party Opt-Out: You can opt-out of third-party cookies by visiting the websites of the third-party services we use.
Cookie Consent
By using our platform, you consent to the use of cookies and other tracking technologies. If you do not consent, you can opt-out by clicking on the "Cookie Consent" button below.
