Accessibility at STREAMIE-

At STREAMIE, we are committed to ensuring that our platform is accessible to all users, including those with disabilities. We strive to provide an inclusive experience that allows everyone to enjoy our content without barriers. This Accessibility section outlines our efforts and the features we have implemented to enhance usability for all.

Our Commitment to Accessibility
Compliance with Standards: We adhere to the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) to ensure our platform meets the accessibility standards set forth by the World Wide Web Consortium (W3C).
Continuous Improvement: We regularly review and update our platform to enhance accessibility features based on user feedback and evolving best practices.
Accessibility Features
Screen Reader Compatibility

Our platform is designed to work seamlessly with popular screen readers, allowing visually impaired users to navigate and access content easily.
Keyboard Navigation

Users can navigate our platform using keyboard shortcuts, making it easier for individuals who cannot use a mouse.
Audio Descriptions

Many of our video titles include audio descriptions that provide additional narration of visual elements, enhancing the viewing experience for users with visual impairments.
Closed Captioning

We offer closed captions for most of our content, enabling users who are deaf or hard of hearing to follow along with the dialogue and sound cues.
Adjustable Text Size and Contrast

Users can adjust text size and contrast settings to suit their visual preferences, improving readability and comfort.
